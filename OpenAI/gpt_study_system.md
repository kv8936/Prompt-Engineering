# 🧠 DAILY GPT STUDY SYSTEM (KISS VERSION)

## 🎯 Goal
Active learning > passive reading

---

## 🔹 STEP 1 — LEARN
Teach me [TOPIC] for practical use.

Give:
- Simple explanation (max 150 words)
- 1 real example
- Common mistakes
- When it fails

---

## 🔹 STEP 2 — TEST (MOST IMPORTANT)
Test me on [TOPIC].
Ask 5 questions one by one.
Do NOT give answers until I respond.

---

## 🔹 STEP 3 — FIX MISTAKES
Here are my answers: [paste]

- Correct them
- Explain ONLY what I got wrong
- Give memory shortcut

---

## 🔹 STEP 4 — SIMPLIFY
Explain [TOPIC] like I’m 12.
Give 3-line memory trick.

---

## 🔹 STEP 5 — NEXT DAY REVISION
Quick revision on [TOPIC]:
- 3 questions
- 1 tricky case

---

## ⚙️ CUSTOM USE

### 🧪 AI / DATA
Add: real-world use + failure cases

### 🇩🇪 GERMAN
Teach me [TOPIC] for B1:
- Rule
- 3 examples
- Common mistakes

Test:
Make me form sentences.

### 💻 CODING / AZURE
Add:
- Mini task
- Expected output

---

## ⚡ 10-MIN VERSION
Explain [TOPIC] in 5 lines.
Ask me 3 questions.

---

## ❌ DON’T DO
- Only reading
- Skipping testing
- No revision
- Jumping topics

---

## 🧠 RULE
Learning = Test + Fix + Repeat
